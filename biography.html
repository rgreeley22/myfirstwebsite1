This is me

I'm a Digital Journalism student at the University of Ottawa. A journalist for the Packet, Communications Specialist for Gee-Gees Football, and a Photographer for Enactus UOttawa.   This field is my passion. The world of reporting is something I've wanted to be in since primary school.   I've never wanted anything else for my career. I started reporting for the Packet and hosting Eastlink TV's CWSHL show when I was 16 years-old.   Moving forward I look at every day as a chance to live my dream, and I'll never be more grateful for that opportunity.
